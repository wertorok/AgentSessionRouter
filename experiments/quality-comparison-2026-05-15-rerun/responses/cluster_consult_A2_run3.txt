

Based on the verified factsheet, the following event types are written to `cluster_events`:

1. `cluster_created`
2. `llm_verifier`
3. `cluster_consult`
4. `cluster_consult_failed`
5. `cluster_refresh_required`
6. `tool_profile_downgraded`
7. `cluster_refresh`
8. `factsheet_stale`

Additionally, `insertClusterFactsheet` can write any of these factsheet status types: `factsheet_static_verified`, `factsheet_partially_static_verified`, `factsheet_llm_verified`, `factsheet_partially_llm_verified`, `factsheet_rejected`, or `factsheet_generated`.

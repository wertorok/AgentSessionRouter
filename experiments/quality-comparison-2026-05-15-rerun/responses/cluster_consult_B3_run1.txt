

## Trust States

**`static_verified`** — occurs when static verification stores at least one valid fact and no facts were rejected.

**`llm_verified`** — occurs when LLM verification accepts all facts and there are no static or LLM rejected facts.

**`partial_llm`** — occurs after LLM verification when at least one fact is rejected but at least one LLM verified fact remains stored.

Key distinction: `static_verified` and `llm_verified` both require zero rejected facts; `partial_llm` is the degraded state when some facts fail LLM verification.

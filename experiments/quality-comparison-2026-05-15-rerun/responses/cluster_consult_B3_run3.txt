

From the verified factsheet:

- **`static_verified`**: Occurs when static verification stores at least one valid fact and no facts were rejected. (fact `trust-static-verified`)

- **`llm_verified`**: Occurs when LLM verification accepts all facts and there are no static or LLM rejected facts. (fact `trust-llm-verified`)

- **`partial_llm`**: Occurs after LLM verification when at least one fact is rejected but at least one LLM verified fact remains stored. (fact `trust-partial-llm`)

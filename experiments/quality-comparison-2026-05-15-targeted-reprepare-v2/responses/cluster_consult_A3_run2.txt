

From the factsheet, there are two layers:

**Low-level `consultCluster` (internal):**
- Marks the factsheet stale via `db.markClusterFactsheetStale`
- Logs `cluster_refresh_required` to cluster_events with `changed_files`
- Returns `CLUSTER_FACTSHEET_STALE` internally

**Caller-facing `cluster_consult` tool (consumes the above error):**
- If `autoRefresh` is enabled: revalidates evidence under a per-cluster lock
- If revalidation succeeds: logs `evidence_revalidated`
- If revalidation fails or `autoRefresh` is disabled: falls back internally to `claude_consult`, logs `cluster_fallback_to_claude_consult`, and returns an answer to the caller

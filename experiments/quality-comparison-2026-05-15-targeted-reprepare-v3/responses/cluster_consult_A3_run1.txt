

**Low-level `consultCluster` function** (from `stale-cluster-consult-behavior`):

- Marks the factsheet stale
- Logs `cluster_refresh_required` to `cluster_events` with `changed_files`
- Returns `CLUSTER_FACTSHEET_STALE` internally

**Caller-facing `cluster_consult` tool** (from `caller-facing-stale-cluster-consult-behavior`):

- Consumes the internal `CLUSTER_FACTSHEET_STALE` error
- **If autoRefresh is enabled**: revalidates evidence under a per-cluster lock
- **If revalidation fails or autoRefresh is disabled**: falls back internally to `claude_consult` and returns an answer to the caller

The complete event sequence (from `caller-facing-stale-event-sequence`) is:

1. `cluster_refresh_required`
2. Then either `evidence_revalidated` (success) or `evidence_revalidation_failed` (strict revalidation failure)
3. Then `cluster_fallback_to_claude_consult` if the fallback answers the caller

**New benchmark decision — BETA-29:**

- **BETA-29**: Factsheet evidence must be revalidated before use; if revalidation cannot prove current facts, the router falls back internally instead of asking the caller to recover.

**Operational meaning:**

When the router needs to use a cached or previously established factsheet to answer a continuity request, it first revalidates that evidence against current state. If revalidation fails or cannot confirm the facts are still true, the router performs an internal fallback — it does not throw the recovery burden back to the caller. The caller experience remains uninterrupted; the router self-corrects quietly.

**Combined benchmark state (ALPHA-17 + BETA-29):**

| Codeword | Rule |
|---|---|
| ALPHA-17 | Router answers caller when Claude is available; monitor telemetry is operator-only |
| BETA-29 | Factsheet evidence revalidated before use; router internal fallback rather than caller recovery |

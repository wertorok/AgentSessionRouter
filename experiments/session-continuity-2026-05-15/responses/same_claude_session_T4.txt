## Benchmark Codewords — Session Memory Recall

**ALPHA-17**: Router MUST answer the caller when Claude is available. Monitor telemetry is operator-only — it does NOT serve as caller-side recovery. Caller gets a live answer; operator gets observability.

**BETA-29**: Factsheet evidence must be revalidated before use. If revalidation cannot prove facts are current, the router falls back internally — it never asks the caller to recover or re-provide information. The router owns the continuity gap, not the caller.

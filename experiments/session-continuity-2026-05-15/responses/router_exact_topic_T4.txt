## Summary of Benchmark Codewords

**ALPHA-17**: The router must answer the caller when Claude is available; monitor telemetry is for the operator, not for caller recovery.

**BETA-29**: Factsheet evidence must be revalidated before use; if revalidation cannot prove current facts, the router falls back internally instead of asking the caller to recover.

Together they define the continuity contract: the router answers directly, and when facts are uncertain, it falls back silently — never burdens the caller with recovery.

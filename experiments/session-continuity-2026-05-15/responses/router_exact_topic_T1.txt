## Benchmark Decision ALPHA-17

- **ALPHA-17**: The router must answer the caller when Claude is available; monitor telemetry is for the operator, not for caller recovery.

**Rationale**: Session continuity is achieved through the router's ability to directly answer and re-establish context with the caller. Monitor/metrics feeds serve observability purposes for the human operator. They are not a recovery mechanism for the caller-facing session state.

**Implication for this benchmark**: When evaluating continuity, the critical path is whether the router can retrieve and restore the session without requiring monitor-level intervention. Caller-facing availability takes precedence over telemetry infrastructure.

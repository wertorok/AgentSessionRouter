

## Trust States

The three states represent progressive verification levels after cluster factsheet preparation:

### `static_verified`
**When:** After static local-file verification, when **zero facts are rejected**.
- The verifier checks that claimed evidence files actually exist on disk
- `src/cluster.ts:133`: `verification.rejectedFacts.length === 0 ? "static_verified" : "partial_static"`
- Acts as a quick-pass cache: fast, no LLM calls, but proves only evidence existence, not semantic correctness

### `partial_llm`
**When:** After LLM verification, when **at least one fact is rejected but at least one LLM-verified fact remains**.
- `src/cluster.ts:222`: `rejectedFacts.length === 0 ? "llm_verified" : "partial_llm"`
- Indicates the LLM那道dismissing some facts as not supported by the evidence
- The factsheet still has usable content, just with reduced confidence

### `llm_verified`
**When:** After LLM verification, when **zero facts are rejected**.
- `src/cluster.ts:222`: `rejectedFacts.length === 0 ? "llm_verified" : "partial_llm"`
- Full semantic check: Claude in no-tools verifier profile confirms all facts are supported by evidence
- Only this state is injected into `bare` consult prompts by default (`src/clusterConsult.ts:56`)

---

There's also `partial_static` (static verification with some rejections) which precedes these three — a transitional state during Phase 1 before LLM verification runs.

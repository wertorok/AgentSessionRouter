  | "static_verified"   // all facts statically verified

**When each occurs:**

| Trust State | Trigger |
|---|---|
| `static_verified` | `cluster_prepare` with `verification_mode: "static"` (default) — all facts pass static checks, zero rejected |
| `llm_verified` | `cluster_prepare` with `verification_mode: "llm"` — LLM verifier promoted every fact to `VERIFIED` |
| `partial_llm` | `cluster_prepare` with `verification_mode: "llm"` — LLM verifier rejected ≥1 facts (some omitted) |

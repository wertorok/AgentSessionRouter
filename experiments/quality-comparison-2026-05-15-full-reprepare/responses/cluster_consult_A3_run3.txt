## Stale Factsheet in `cluster_consult`

The behavior differs between the low-level detection and the caller-facing tool:

### Low-level `consultCluster` (when scoped evidence files are stale)
- Marks the factsheet stale
- Logs `cluster_refresh_required` to `cluster_events` with `changed_files`
- Returns `CLUSTER_FACTSHEET_STALE` internally (does not answer the caller)

### Caller-facing `cluster_consult` tool
Consumes the `CLUSTER_FACTSHEET_STALE` error with this decision tree:

1. **If `autoRefresh` is enabled**: Revalidates evidence under a per-cluster lock (`cluster-evidence-revalidation`).
2. **If revalidation succeeds**: Logs `evidence_revalidated` to `cluster_events` — continues with the answer.
3. **If revalidation fails or `autoRefresh` is disabled**: Falls back internally to `claude_consult`, logs `cluster_fallback_to_claude_consult` to `cluster_events`, and returns the answer to the caller.

### Complete event sequence for stale factsheet
1. `cluster_refresh_required`
2. Then either `evidence_revalidated` (success) or `evidence_revalidation_failed` (strict revalidation failure)
3. Then `cluster_fallback_to_claude_consult` (if the fallback path answers)

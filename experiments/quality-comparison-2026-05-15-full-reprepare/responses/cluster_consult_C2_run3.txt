

## Likely Untested Failure Mode

**Failure:** `--bare --tools ""` silently succeeds on an older Claude version that accepts the args but produces degraded or non-standard output (e.g., missing session IDs, malformed JSON).

**Detection:** Watch for `bare_probe_failed` in cluster_events. Additionally, assert that consult outputs include valid `sessionId` and conform to expected response schema — not just that the call completes without error.

The factsheet documents `bare_probe_failed` as the router's built-in sentinel for this path, but it likely only tests the probe itself, not the downstream output shape when args are accepted by an incompatible version.

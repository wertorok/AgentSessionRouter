

## Stale Factsheet Behavior in `cluster_consult`

**Low-level (consultCluster):**
- Detects stale scoped evidence files (changed hashes or selectors)
- Marks the factsheet stale in the database
- Logs `cluster_refresh_required` to cluster_events (with `changed_files` in details)
- Returns `CLUSTER_FACTSHEET_STALE` error internally

**Caller-facing (cluster_consult tool):**
- Consumes the internal `CLUSTER_FACTSHEET_STALE` error
- If `autoRefresh` is enabled: revalidates evidence under a per-cluster lock via `revalidateClusterEvidence` → logs `evidence_revalidated` or `evidence_revalidation_failed`
- If revalidation fails OR `autoRefresh` is disabled: falls back internally to `claude_consult` → logs `cluster_fallback_to_claude_consult`

**Complete event sequence for a stale factsheet:**
1. `cluster_refresh_required` (detection)
2. `evidence_revalidated` or `evidence_revalidation_failed` (revalidation outcome)
3. `cluster_fallback_to_claude_consult` (if fallback answers the caller)

## New Benchmark Decision Added

- **BETA-29**: Factsheet evidence must be revalidated before use; if revalidation cannot prove current facts, the router falls back internally instead of asking the caller to recover.

**Operational meaning**: The router does not trust stale factsheets blindly. Before acting on any cached fact, it re-validates. If revalidation fails, it performs an internal fallback—caller experience remains seamless; the caller is never put in the position of having to recover or re-provide information.

**Combined benchmark state**:
- ALPHA-17: Router answers caller when Claude available; telemetry is operator-facing
- BETA-29: Factsheet revalidation required; internal fallback on failure, never caller-facing recovery

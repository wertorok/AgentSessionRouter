## BETA-29 — New Benchmark Decision

- **Codeword**: **BETA-29**
- **Operational Meaning**: Factsheet evidence must be revalidated before use. If revalidation cannot prove the facts are current, the router falls back internally rather than asking the caller to recover.

Combined benchmark state:

- **ALPHA-17**: Router answers the caller when Claude is available; monitor telemetry is operator-only, not caller recovery.
